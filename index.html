The Rasengan is a spinning ball of chakra formed and held in the palm of the user's hand, which is only known to a handful of shinobi.

The Chidori is a high concentration of lightning chakra channelled around the user's hand.

The Shadow Clone Technique was created by Tobirama Senju. This jutsu allows the user to create one or more copy of themselves.

Fire Style: Fireball Jutsu is native to the Uchiha Clan. The user converts kneaded chakra inside their body into fire, and expells it from their mouth in a massive orb of roaring flame.

Sage Mode is an empowered state that can be entered by blending natural energy with one's chakra, creating senjutsu chakra.

Nindō (忍道) is a personal rule that each shinobi lives by.

It is their way of ninja life, their motto, belief, or "dream". A ninja's nindō can be anything, no matter how immoral their personal point of view is. Most often Naruto Uzumaki proclaims his ninja way, to be: "I'm not gonna run away and I never go back on my word, that is my nindō! My ninja way! According to Tobi, the nindō of the Uchiha clan is to exact revenge against their hated enemies.
